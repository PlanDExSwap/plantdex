About Us

DeFi is one of the places to be right now as it provides decentralized financial services, that was originally offered by the centralized finance institutions. PlantDEx is one of the latest protocol offering cutting edge DeFi services. With a Governance token $SEED who's holders have the right to govern and make decisions concerning the affairs of the PlantDEx ecosystem, $Seed can also be put to work through planting (staking) in the $TREE pool to earn $TREEs and added to a liquidity pool which you also earn $TREE as Reward. $TREE will in future be the ultimate token for purchasing and trading agrobased stock. With $TREE, users will be able to Transplant (fruit pool staking) with ease and confidence and also earn fruit pool tokens (These are tokens from partner or community projects).

Yield Farming on PlantDEx

Yield Farming, also referred to as liquidity mining is a way to generate rewards with cryptocurrency holdings. It's simply the process of locking up cryptocurrencies and getting rewards.

On PlantDEx our reward token for liquidity mining is $TREE token which is gotten from locking our governance token $SEED with other liquidity provider tokens available in our liquidity pool, 70% of our $TREE token block reward have been allocated to liquidity farmers.

Staking on PlantDEx

Staking is the process of actively participating in transaction validation on proof of stake(POS) blockchain. This is very similar to mining. On PlantDEx two staking pools have been made available, which are $TREE staking pool, which involves the staking of $SEED our governance token to get $TREEs, and fruit pool which involves staking of $TREEs to get fruits(tokens from partner or community project).

The process of staking $SEED to get $TREE is tag planting, while staking $TREEs to get fruits is transplanting. 30% of free block rewards has been allocated to $SEED stakers in the $TREE pool.
